iOS-8-Bootstrap beta 1.0

A sleek, elegant step up from iOS 7 Bootstrap.

iOS 8 UI with CSS3, JavaScript & HTML5 remakes of Switches, Control Center, animated bubbles, modals and more. Just add this to boostrap and give your website the depth and vitality of iOS 8's great look and features.
